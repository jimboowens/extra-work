Green Cleaning

MCS/Green Clean is a commercial cleaning program that is designed to increase the health and indoor air quality of our buildings for the benefit of our employees, tenants and the environment.

MCS is committed to developing and maintaining an environment that enhances human health and fosters a transition toward sustainability. The MCS/Green Clean program is a commercial cleaning program that is designed to increase the health and quality of our buildings for the benefit of our employees, tenants and the environment. MCS is committed to continuous improvement in:
    • Demonstrating institutional practices that promote sustainability, including measures to increase efficiency and use of renewable resources, and to decrease production of waste and hazardous materials, both in MCS’s own operations and in those of its suppliers.
    • Promoting health, productivity and safety of the MCS community through design and maintenance of the built environment.
    • Enhancing the health of our building’s ecosystems.
    • Developing planning tools to enable comparative analysis of sustainability implications and to support long-term economic, environmental and socially responsible decision-making.
    • Encouraging environmental inquiry and institutional learning throughout the company.
    • Establishing indicators for sustainability that will enable monitoring reporting and continuous improvement.

Sustainability

Commitment

MCS has incorporated a number of sustainability initiatives in our buildings and programs and will continue to expand and develop environmental best practices in our operations across the city. We believe sustainability is not only good corporate citizenry, but also good business.

Culture

MCS is committed to a sustainability-minded culture in every division and at every level. Our own client sites will be managed according to sustainability best practices, with a focus on the following efforts:

• Employee training and education
• Implementation of MCS/Green Clean • Energy conservation • Indoor air quality • Resource conservation • Responsible procurement
• Transportation • Waste reduction • Water conservation

Innovation

MCS will strive to be a sustainability innovator. We will test promising sustainable technologies in real-life circumstances in our portfolio to identify and implement the most effective products and services. Further, we will seek opportunities to effect change on a portfolio-wide basis and take advantage of our financial and operational strength to pursue creative solutions.

About Us

MCS is a Chattanooga-based commercial green cleaning company that includes not only the use of Green Seal and similar green certified products-its takes a holistic approach to sustainability and incorporates our unique philophy that health is directly tied to our tenants/clients satisfaction. This includes the use of compliant equipment, procedures, training, occupant issues, recycling and more and is backed by our documented Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) witten with the US Green Building Councils, LEED-EB certification as its foundation.

MCS has been providing the downtown area with exceptional services. When Erwin Ovalle founded MCS in January 2014, he saw the need and opportunity for a green cleaning service that would provide reliable, consistent and quality service to commercial and residential buildings, offices, medical facilities, schools, banks, and hotels. MCS continues to bolster its reputation by providing continuous training to its employees

This allows us to stay abreast of changes in the field, thus boosting the quality of service. MCS continues to follow Mr. Erwin's example of placing the customer above all else. Employees from all aspects of the firm understand this policy. This sole factor allows the MCS reputation to remain strong in the cleaning industry, allowing the firm to stay strong and continue to grow.

Go Green

Green Cleaning can be defined as, "cleaning that protects health without harming the environment." At Management Cleaning Service, Inc., we believe it will be a requirement for all building contractors in the future, but we are not waiting. We believe that we are to be good stewards of the planet and should be responsible in helping preserve it. We live in a beautiful city, and we want to make sure that, at MSC, we do our part to keep it that way.

We partner with several green-supportive suppliers to help our clients do their part for the planet and their own environment. Our ultimate goal is to keep your facility clean and protect human health!
